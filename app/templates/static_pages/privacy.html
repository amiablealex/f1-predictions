Privacy

Short version: this is a hobby project run by one person. Your data isn't going anywhere it shouldn't.

What's stored

  • Your email. Used for login and password reset. That's it.
  • Your username. Visible to other members of leagues you join.
  • A hash of your password. The plain text is never seen or stored. If you forget it, you'll need to reset — not even the admin can recover it.
  • Your predictions and scores. Visible to other members of your leagues, because that's the whole point.

What isn't

  • No tracking. No analytics. No ad networks. Nothing on this site reports back to a third party about what you clicked.
  • Your email isn't shared, sold, used for marketing, or rented to anyone wanting to sell you a sim rig.
  • Cookies are strictly the ones needed to keep you logged in. Nothing else.

Where the data lives

The app and database are hosted on Railway (US infrastructure). Backups are taken nightly. Password reset emails are sent via Resend; no other email is sent from this app.

Your rights

You can delete your account at any time from the Account page — it removes you and your predictions completely. If you'd rather have a copy of your data first, or there's anything else you'd like to ask, email kitsniff.app@gmail.com.

Contact

Anything privacy-shaped: kitsniff.app@gmail.com.

This policy applies under UK and EU data protection law. Last updated: 15 May 2026.
