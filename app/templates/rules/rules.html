How to play

Predict each round's results. Closer to the truth = more points.

The basics

Every Grand Prix is a "round". For each round you predict a set of outcomes; once the deadline passes, your predictions lock and you watch as points roll in across the weekend.

Deadline: {{ deadline_offset_minutes }} minutes before the first scoring session of the weekend (sprint qualifying on a sprint weekend, otherwise main qualifying).

Sprint weekends

Predictions on a sprint weekend lock 1 hour before sprint qualifying on Friday.

  • Sprint race top 3: +{{ cfg.sprint_top3_correct }} exact, +{{ cfg.sprint_top3_one_off }} off by one.

Race extras

  • Fastest lap: +{{ cfg.fastest_lap_correct }} if you pick the right driver.
  • DNF count: +{{ cfg.dnf_count_correct }} exact, +{{ cfg.dnf_count_one_off }} off by one.
  • Places gained: pick any driver. Score = grid position − finish position. P4 → P2 finish = +2; P4 → P10 = -6. No cap, no floor.
    DNFs count as last classified position; DNS scores 0.

Substitutions

If a driver is replaced for a round (illness, reserve called up), points are awarded by seat — i.e. picking Hamilton means picking "whoever drives car 44 this round."

Leaderboards

  • Total points: sum of every prediction across the season.
  • Head-to-head: 1 point per round won within your league. Ties share the win.
